Country Style

Modern country style of interior design is an idyllic classic. Stepping away from a purely traditional country design style; modern country allows for more playful and nuanced aspects along with minimal notes.

And since theres a series of elements that articulate the perfect country-style space, we break down the look of modern country decorating along with some clever country decorating ideas on a budget for inspiration.

Modern Flat

Within this flat, a great deal of personality comes from the dramatic textures rather than any extraneous decoration - creative blend of modern and classic materials with just the right hint of wood thrown in for warmth.

Alternating matte and gloss checkerboard wall panels offer a subtle style that softens the interior. Modern Flat is all about practicality.
